Story

Kingfisher Kitchens started in 2009

I studied Mechanical draughting after school but felt that it lacked the practical side of woodwork that I enjoyed. I worked in the clothing industry for 10 years before turning my hobby into my full time job.

Moving to Cape Town in 2009 meant that I had an opportunity to change carreer paths and follow 2 of my passions in life. Technical drawings and working with my hands have always been 2 things I really enjoy. Starting a Kitchen renovation company gave me opportuinity to use my drawing and practical woodwork skills on a daily basis.

We started small with me doing all the installations during the day and quoting at night. Eventually we got to the point of hiring an artisan to come in and work on site with me in 2011, and a second one later that year. By 2012 we had a staff compliment of 2 full time employees and me filling in the gaps on site. This gave me the opportunity to expand the business and be able to bring in more clients during the day. Site consultations and quotes could be done during the day as my staff were on site doing the installations. By 2016 we had a staff compliment of 3 full time employees.

Water Protection System

All floor standing units are supported by adjustable plastic legs, concealed by cabinet skirting. In the event of a water leak, these legs protect the cabinet carcasses from damage, limiting damage to the easily replaceable skirting only.
